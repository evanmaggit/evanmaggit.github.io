Shrimp Tempura

Shrimp Tempura rolls are a craving of rice, spicy mayo, fish, a wasabi/soy sauce combination, and of course... cooked shrimp.

Ingredients

  • Sushi rice
  • Shrimp tempura
  • Rice seasoning
  • Seaweed sheets
  • Avocado
  • Cucumber
  • Sriracha mayo
  • Sesame seeds

Directions

  1. How to Cook Rice:
    1. Cook rice in water for 5-10 minutes.
    2. Pour rice bowl and add rice seasoning.
  2. Make Shrimp Tempura:
    1. Wash, pat the shrimp dry, and straighten shrimp.
    2. Trim shrimp tails and clean them.
    3. Dip shrimps in a bowl of flour.
    4. Cook floured shrimps in small batches for 2-3 minutes at a time.
  3. Make Shrimp Tempura Rolls:
    1. Use a bamboo mat over a cutting board, place a seaweed sheet on it, and prepare rice, avocado, and cucumber.
    2. Spread a thin layer of rice all on it.
    3. Place another seaweed sheet over it, add avocado and cucumber horizontally, and roll it into a cylinder.
    4. Use the mat to shape the roll, and cut it into six to eight pieces.
    5. Add sriracha mayo over it with seasoning, and it's... ready to serve.
